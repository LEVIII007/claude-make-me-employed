# Job Search Preferences

## Primary target

- Role family: Software Engineer
- Experience target: 0-1 years
- Best-fit angle: backend, distributed systems, infra, platform, and general software engineering

## Internship filter

- Consider internships only if monthly compensation is at least INR 80,000

## Current positioning

- Fresher / recent graduate
- Strong internship experience
- Comfortable applying to roles that say 0-1 years
- Also worth checking some 1-2 year roles when the description looks fresher-friendly

## What to prioritize

- Backend-heavy roles
- General software engineering roles
- Founding engineer / early engineering roles only when expectations are still realistic for a fresher
- Companies where internship and project depth can compensate for lower full-time experience

## Company tier targets

- In scope: Enterprise / MNC
- In scope: Large Public Company
- In scope: Late-Stage Startup / Unicorn
- In scope: Growth-Stage Startup / Scale-Up

## Company tier priority

- Primary: Growth-Stage Startup / Scale-Up
- Primary: Late-Stage Startup / Unicorn
- Secondary: Large Public Company
- Secondary: Enterprise / MNC

## What each tier means for this search

- Enterprise / MNC: large multinational engineering organizations with formal early-career hiring, strong brand value, and slower hiring loops
- Large Public Company: large listed companies with stable teams and stronger process, even when they are less campus-style than classic MNC pipelines
- Late-Stage Startup / Unicorn: strong product companies with startup upside, better compensation potential, and enough scale to have real junior roles
- Growth-Stage Startup / Scale-Up: smaller but proven startups where strong internship depth can still stand out and the role can move faster

## Tier-specific fit rules

- Enterprise / MNC: prioritize only when the role is clearly new-grad, graduate, associate, or 0-1 years
- Large Public Company: prioritize backend, platform, and general software engineer roles with realistic junior scope
- Late-Stage Startup / Unicorn: prioritize SDE I, backend engineer, platform engineer, or infrastructure-leaning roles where internships can count toward experience
- Growth-Stage Startup / Scale-Up: prioritize teams that value ownership, shipping ability, and projects, but avoid roles that expect a fully independent 2-3 year engineer from day one

## Search allocation

- Rough weekly mix: 30 percent Growth-Stage Startup / Scale-Up
- Rough weekly mix: 30 percent Late-Stage Startup / Unicorn
- Rough weekly mix: 20 percent Large Public Company
- Rough weekly mix: 20 percent Enterprise / MNC

## Tier guardrails

- Do not chase very early-stage startups unless the JD is unusually fresher-friendly
- Do not over-prioritize brand if the role itself is not backend, platform, infra, or strong general SWE
- Do not spend too much time on MNC roles with campus-only pipelines unless there is a live off-campus path
- Treat 1-2 year startup roles as valid stretch shots only when internships, projects, or the wording clearly keep the bar realistic

## Compensation preference

- Target full-time compensation: 18-20 LPA+ when compensation is discussed explicitly

## What to capture during search

- Company
- Company tier
- Role title
- Source
- Location / remote policy
- Experience asked
- Compensation clues
- Tech stack clues
- Freshness of posting
- Why this role fits
