# Job Search Shortlist - 2026-06-29

Built from the ATS-board search method (`site:` queries against Lever, Ashby, Greenhouse, and Workday-style boards) using the preferences in `job-search-preferences.md`.

## Search assumptions

- Geography: India-first search, with Bengaluru and Remote India prioritized
- Role focus: backend, platform, infra, and general software engineering
- Seniority: strongest focus on 0-1 years, with selective 1-2 or 1-3 year stretch roles when the JD still looks internship-friendly
- Tier bias: growth-stage and late-stage product companies first, then larger/public companies with junior-friendly openings
