## Practical filter for the next pass

Prioritize roles when the JD has at least two of these signals:
- `Associate`, `SDE I`, `Software Engineer I`, `Graduate`, `New Grad`
- `1+ years`, `1-2 years`, or `1-3 years`
- explicit mention that internships, projects, or academic background can count
- backend APIs, distributed systems, infra, platform, data infrastructure, or reliability themes

Skip or deprioritize when the JD has two or more of these:
- `3+ years` with no fresher-friendly wording
- leadership / mentoring expectations for a supposedly junior title
- highly full-stack and design-heavy role with weak backend depth
- very early-stage startup plus unclear onboarding support
