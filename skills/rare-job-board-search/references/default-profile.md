# Default Profile

Use this file when the workspace does not provide local preference files.

## Candidate target

- Role family: software engineer
- Best-fit angle: backend, distributed systems, infrastructure, platform, and general software engineering
- Experience target: 0-1 years
- Stretch range: some 1-2 year roles when internships or projects can plausibly substitute
- Positioning: fresher or recent graduate with strong internship experience

## Search priorities

- Prefer backend-heavy or general SWE roles first
- Keep platform and infrastructure roles in scope when the JD still reads junior-friendly
- Keep founding-engineer roles only when expectations stay realistic for a fresher
- Prioritize growth-stage startups and late-stage startups first, then public companies and MNCs
- Avoid very early-stage startup roles unless the JD is unusually fresher-friendly

## Geography and work mode

- Prioritize India, remote-in-India, and common India tech hubs
- Start with Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Gurgaon, Noida, and Chennai when a city sweep helps
- Treat location-locked onsite roles as weaker fits unless the rest of the JD is unusually strong

## Compensation guardrails

- Internship floor: INR 80,000 per month when compensation is visible
- Full-time preference: 18-20 LPA or above when compensation is visible

## What to capture

- Company
- Company tier
- Role title
- Source
- Location or remote policy
- Experience asked
- Compensation clues
- Tech stack clues
- Freshness of posting
- Why the role fits
